    • Are You at Risk

    Do you look at a screen for over 2 hours a day? This includes at work, on your phone, or a tablet not just a computer. The amount of time looking at a screen can cause your eyes to strain weakening the muscles in your eyes and causing vision problems. This can also worsen any preexisting eye conditions that you may have. The risk is having computer eye syndrome. The symptoms are as follows: burning sensation, redness, dryness, tearing, neck stiffnes, neck pain, sholder pain, headache, backache, blurring of vision, double vision, presbyopia, myopia, slowness of focus change. All of these symptoms are what one can experinece when looking at a screen for too long. It is estimated that more than 60% of Americans experience the symptoms of computer eye syndrome.

    Benefits

    Some of the many benefits fo decreasing your screen time are less damage to your eyesight. Less risk of addiction to your device. This can help you form connections with those around you and going outside. Being outside will increase vitamin d in your system increasing the "happy" chemicals in your brain (dopamine and seratonin). You will not only develop one skill of being good with computers or video games you can increase the skills you have and learn how to complete new tasks.

    Video Games and Vision

    Video games are one of the most common pastimes and stress releivers for people.This passtime can be fun, and carefree. But there is a part of this passtime that does need to be discussed. The effects it can have on your eyes. Staring at the screen for extended amounts of time can cause problems with your eyesight and worsen any eye conditions that you may have. The most common issue is Computer vision/eye syndrome. This can cause issues that are permanant for the rest of your life. It is important to limit screen time and protect your eyes.
