My Story

When I was younger I would play vidoe games for at least six hours a day. I slowly noticed a change in my eyesight mood and knew I had to make a change. I learned the longer I was looking at the screen I would suffer long term affects. I was addicted to video games and also needed glasses for the damage to my eyesight. Now I want to help educate and help others who are addicted to video games and help them with their addiction.
